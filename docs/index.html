Book Overview

This book introduces a formula for life that resolves all the problems humanity faces-something entirely new in history, solved through mathematics. From the economic, political, and social issues of humanity to the individual’s spiritual and lifestyle concerns, this formula addresses it all.

Auther Lee Sangdae has dedicated himself since 1961 (at age 25) to finding a formula that creates a world without bloodshed, tears, and sighs. Among the numerous culminations reached, he has encapsulated key concepts in “Higher Consciousness - Working Thinking.”

Individuals who understand and apply this formula in their own lives-knowing how their thoughts and actions simultaneously benefit their nation, ethnicity, and humanity-will be the foundation for eternal peace and harmony for humankind.

Therefore, educators, philosophers, sociologists, those seeking moral recovery, teachers and parents striving for character education, as well as religious or political leaders, are particularly encouraged to prioritize reading this book.

What is Higher Consciousness-Working Thinking Education?

Within the pages of the book

Just as we need to know the formula when solving a math problem at school or doing something in the world and can easily get the answer by substituting the problems in our lives into that formula to solve them, we also need to know the formula for solving problems in our lives. There is a formula for the creation and death of all things just like our lives have a formula for being born and living on this earth.

It is an education that awakens the duality of the gap between thoughts(mind) that want to live happily and actions that have nothing to do with happiness.

It is an education that helps you understand the difference between mathematical thinking and non-mathematical behavior, and the gap between scientific thinking and non-scientific behavior.

It is an education that allows you to run the country while minimizing the people’s tax burden.

It is an education that teaches you to act without having to worry about what others think of you or doing anything that might cause others to interfere with your affairs.

It is an education allowing each individual clearly see that the chain of corruption is so deeply rooted from our individual consciousness to every corner of the consciousness of the nation and society as a whole and that the problem can no longer be solved through physical or institutional method and that it must begin with their own internal revolution(『The Book of Changes』: 革卦).

It is an education that teaches you that the result of yesterday(yesterday’s way of thinking, life attitude) is today(today’s way of thinking, life attitude), and lets you thoroughly realize that the result of today is tomorrow, and that your position and consciousness in life tomorrow will be determined only by whether you are truthful, sincere, diligent, honest, thorough, and accurate this very moment.

Because the Era of “우리(we; not individualism like ’You’re you, I’m me)” which means Hwan-in(桓因: the origin of light), and Hongik(弘益: Greater benefit for all) will begin again. Otherwise, this era of confusion will not end.

Publisher-provided book introduction

Author Lee Sangdae’s ‘Higher Consciousness-Working Thinking’ (Subtitle: Great Perfect Cycle New Start from Korea; The Foundation of AI Consciousness) presents a groundbreaking approach never seen in history, unraveling humanity’s consciousness development through mathematical formulas.

Particularly, introducing the innovative concept of “Life Mathematics” pioneered by the author, this work introduces a new method of expressing human consciousness in the language of mathematics. It offers a clear-cut approach to resolving complex life problems in a simple and lucid manner, akin to mathematical formulas.

Considering the pivotal role of human consciousness and behavioral shifts in shaping the advancement, diretion, and ethical standards of Artificial Intelligence(AI), this novel concept embodies crucial principles not only in “the evolution and growth of human consciousness” but also in the “development, provision, and utilization of AI for humanity.”

Moreover, by advocating the application of life formulas to one’s own life, the book illustrates how individuals can joyfully and happily embrace diverse choices and their outcomes based on their personal traits and preferences. It further delves into addressing the problems faced by humanity as a whole, prompting readers to contemplate deeply and pose essential questions.

Through this book, the author aims to offer solutions unprecedented in this era of chaos and confusion, hoping that readers find guidance in leading a fulfilling and joyful life after reading, and ultimately discover the right path for themselves amid the challenges humanity faces.
